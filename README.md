## Welcome to my corner of the internet

I'm the captain/founder of FIRST Robotics Team 7461, Sushi Squad, and work a lot on design/fabrication, along with controls. In addition, I'm working on a few personal projects, the most notable being my self-driving car project, which is currently just an implementation of a potential field algorithm to guide a vehicle around obstacles, but some targets include finding a better way to adapt it to ackermann-steered vehicles, and using the potential field to plan short paths that take into account vehicle heading, instead of just janking together a solution.
